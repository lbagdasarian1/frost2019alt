Will ADA parking be available?

Yes. Individuals with a valid, state-issued disabled person parking placard or license plate will have access to parking on Roth way between Palm and Lasuen. We will update this page with a parking map displaying this location closer to the event. For questions, please call phone: 650-725-0326 or e-mail: disability.access@stanford.edu.


Will first aid be available?

Yes. First Aid will be available inside and around the venue. IN CASE OF EMERGENCY: DIAL 911 AND Seek assistance from staff and security personnel in the venue.


Will a lost and found be available?

Yes. We will publish details closer to the event. After the concert, unclaimed items will be taken to: Stanford Department of Public Safety: 650-725-0326. Stanford University is not responsible for any lost or stolen items.

Do I need to bring my Stanford ID?

Yes. If you are a Stanford student you must bring your Stanford ID. Your Stanford student ticket will be pre-loaded to your SUID. IDs not matching with the individual will be confiscated. Please have your ticket with you at all times. Any ticketing issues can be addressed at the Box Office.


Will re-entry be allowed?

No. You may leave the event at any time but re-entry will be prohibited.


Is there an age minimum?

No. All guests require a ticket for entry.


What are the set times?

Doors open at 5:00 PM and the show starts at 6:00 PM. Specific set times will not be released and are subject to change.


What can I bring to the event?

Attendees must abide by the clear bag policy. Guidelines can be found here. There will be additional regulations TBD.


Will alcohol or smoking be allowed?

Neither. You may be required to open your beverage containers for review prior to entering the venue. Persons in possession of alcohol or who are visibly intoxicated will be denied entry, or are subject to removal from the venue, with no refund.
No smoking of ANY kind (including e-cigarettes) will be tolerated inside or in the immediate vicinity outside of the venue, within 30 feet of the venue, and within 30 feet of ticket lines. Those who are found smoking inside the venue may be subject to removal with no refund.
